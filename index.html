About Me

Welcome to my personal website. I am a mathematician specializing in probability theory and spectral theory. I am currently a research assistant at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, where I conduct research and teach advanced courses in mathematics.

My work focuses on solving complex problems in pure mathematics, with applications in artificial intelligence and computational science.

Teaching Experience

I have been teaching mathematics at both undergraduate and graduate levels for over 5 years. My teaching philosophy emphasizes conceptual understanding, problem-solving skills, and real-world applications of mathematical theory.
